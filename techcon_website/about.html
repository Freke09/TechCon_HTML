History

TechCon is a conference focused on technical advancements and solutions, particularly in the field of high voltage maintenance and asset management. It has evolved from being part of the UK Radio Festival to an independently run event by TBC Media Ltd. since 2016, focusing on supporting the UK radio and audio industry's technical community. TechCon North America, another conference, has been running since 1996, providing a platform for experts in the utility and power generation industry to discuss technical developments and solutions for the aging electrical grid infrastructure.

Mission

TechCon conferences, such as the one held in Chicago, aim to educate, foster networking, drive innovation, and showcase excellence in specific fields like vacuum coating and surface engineering. These events serve as platforms for sharing knowledge, connecting professionals, and promoting the adoption of new technologies. Key driving principles include providing in-depth technological expertise, offering abundant networking opportunities, and delivering an extensive educational program.
